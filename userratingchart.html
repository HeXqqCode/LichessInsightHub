How User Account Rating Statistics Work on Lichess

The User Account Rating Statistics on Lichess provide detailed insights into a player's performance. It includes data on rating trends, best-performing time controls, and achievements within a specified rating range.

This feature allows players to track their progress, identify strengths and weaknesses, and set goals for improvement.

Rights of User Account Rating Statistics on Lichess

Users have the right to access and analyze their personal rating statistics. Lichess ensures transparency by providing accurate and up-to-date information, empowering players to make informed decisions about their chess journey.

The user-friendly interface makes it easy for players to interpret and leverage their rating statistics for strategic gameplay.
