Understanding Your Rating on Lichess

How Lichess User Account Rating Statistics Work

The Lichess User Account Rating Statistics provide a comprehensive overview of your chess performance. It includes data on your rating changes, game outcomes, and performance trends over time.

Users can analyze their statistics to identify strengths, weaknesses, and track their progress. It serves as a valuable tool for self-improvement and strategic gameplay.

Rights of Lichess User Account Rating Statistics

Lichess users have the right to access and analyze their personal rating statistics. It empowers players to make informed decisions about their gameplay, set goals, and measure their improvement over different time periods.

The rating statistics system aligns with Lichess's commitment to transparency and user-driven improvements, contributing to a positive and competitive online chess community.
