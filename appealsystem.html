Navigating the Appeals Process on Lichess

How the Appeal System Works on Lichess

The Lichess Appeal System provides users with a mechanism to contest account-related decisions, including bans. It ensures a fair and transparent process for users to appeal and present their case to the Lichess team.

Users can utilize the appeal system to request a review of their account status, presenting evidence and context to support their appeal.

Rights of an Appeal on Lichess

The appeal system grants users the right to contest account-related actions taken by Lichess. It aims to provide a balanced and just resolution, considering user input and additional context before making final decisions.

Lichess is committed to maintaining a fair and respectful online chess community, and the appeal system plays a crucial role in upholding these principles.
