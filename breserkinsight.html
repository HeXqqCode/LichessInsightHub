How the Berserk System Works

The Berserk system on Lichess allows players to take risks and gain rewards. When a player chooses to Berserk, they commit to completing the game in half the usual time but gain an extra point if they win.

This feature is designed to add an extra layer of excitement and challenge to games, encouraging faster play and strategic decision-making.

Purpose of Berserk in Lichess

The primary purpose of the Berserk option is to promote dynamic and aggressive play. It rewards players who are willing to take on a time disadvantage for the chance to secure an additional point in the game.

Berserking can be a strategic choice, especially in faster time controls, where quick decision-making and time management can make a significant impact on the outcome of the game.

Why Lichess Uses the Berserk System

Lichess incorporates the Berserk system to diversify gameplay and offer players an option to inject more excitement into their matches. It aligns with the platform's commitment to providing a dynamic and engaging chess experience.

The Berserk system contributes to the unique and innovative features that make Lichess stand out among online chess platforms.
