Connecting Chess Enthusiasts through Chat

How the Chat System Works on Lichess

The Lichess Chat System allows users to engage in real-time conversations with other chess enthusiasts. It serves as a platform for players to discuss strategies, analyze games, and build a sense of community within the Lichess ecosystem.

Users can send direct messages, participate in group chats, and enhance their overall chess experience through collaborative interactions.

Rights of a Chat User on Lichess

Chat users on Lichess have the right to communicate respectfully and share their insights with the community. The platform promotes a positive and inclusive environment, ensuring that users can enjoy chess-related discussions while adhering to community guidelines.

Lichess actively moderates chat content to maintain a welcoming space for players of all levels and backgrounds.
