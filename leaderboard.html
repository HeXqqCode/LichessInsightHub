How the Leaderboard Works on Lichess

The Lichess Leaderboard is a dynamic system that showcases the top-performing players based on their ratings. It provides an overview of the most skilled players within a specific rating range, fostering healthy competition and recognition.

Users can explore the leaderboard to identify strong opponents, track their progress, and strive to climb higher in the rankings.

Rights of a Leaderboard on Lichess

The leaderboard grants players the right to be recognized for their achievements and skills. It offers a platform for users to showcase their chess prowess and compete among the best.

Lichess ensures fair play and accuracy in the leaderboard, creating a competitive yet respectful environment for players of all levels.
