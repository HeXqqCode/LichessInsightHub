How Lichess Donation System Works

The Lichess Donation System allows users to contribute funds to support the platform's continued development and maintenance. Donations play a crucial role in keeping Lichess free and open-source for millions of players worldwide.

When you make a donation, you're contributing to server costs, feature enhancements, and the overall improvement of the Lichess experience.

Purpose of Lichess Donate and Get Patron Wings

Lichess encourages users to become Patrons by donating. Becoming a Patron not only supports the platform but also unlocks special features, such as the coveted Patron Wings badge.

Patron Wings signify your support for Lichess, and patrons often enjoy early access to new features, recognition on the platform, and a sense of pride in contributing to the chess community.

Why Lichess Uses Donation Despite Being Free and Open-Source

While Lichess is committed to remaining free and open-source forever, running a platform of this scale incurs significant operational costs. Donations from users ensure the platform's sustainability, allowing Lichess to maintain its high-quality service and provide an ad-free and user-friendly chess environment.

How to Become a Lichess Patron

Becoming a Lichess Patron is easy. Simply click on the "Donate" button, choose your contribution amount, and follow the instructions to complete the donation process. Once confirmed, you'll be recognized as a Patron with the exclusive Patron Wings badge.
